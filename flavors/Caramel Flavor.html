Caramel

Caramel is a liquid sugar that it is put some sugar into hot water and let the sugar coke then those caramel taste crispy, sweet, and a little bit coke. Sometimes it is used to make some color on the cake and pudding. Now, we use caramel into our ice cream to make it tastier. Enjoy the crispy taste and sweet dance in your mouth.
